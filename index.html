Powerful Features for Smart Investors

Everything you need to source, evaluate, and close deals faster

📍

Property Search & Database

Access MLS and public records. Filter by investment criteria, ROI targets, location, and asset class. Find deals that match your portfolio strategy.

💰

Valuation & Appraisal

Get instant property valuations using automated valuation models (AVM) and comparable property analysis. Generate professional valuation reports in minutes.

📊

Market Intelligence

Heat maps, neighborhood analytics, market trends, and forecasts. Understand pricing patterns, rental yields, and growth opportunities by submarket.

🎯

Deal Pipeline Management

Organize and score sourced deals. Track from sourcing through closing with automated workflows, notifications, and deal analytics.

📈

Financial Modeling

Pro-forma models, financing calculators, cash flow projections, and ROI analysis. Model scenarios in seconds and compare investment options.

⚖️

Legal & Documentation

Document templates, e-signature integration, legal checklists, and attorney/title company referrals to streamline your closing process.
